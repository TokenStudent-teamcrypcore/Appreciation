Why Student Coin?

STC is a reliable, fundamental, long-term project based on academic values.
STC allows you to create and manage your personal, start-up, NFT and DeFi token.
STC Token holders will receive cyclical bonuses generated by our products.
STC is building a global community that connects both token holders and students, just like social media at the beginning.
STC Token works as the central and reference currency. By Premium Holder Program, the holder can benefit from airdrops for Student Coin Users.
We have created the STC Academy to promote tokenization and educate about economics, finance and new technologies.
